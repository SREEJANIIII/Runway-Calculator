# Startup Runway Calculator

A futuristic, mobile-friendly financial runway calculator designed to help entrepreneurs quickly assess how long their startup can survive based on current cash reserves and monthly burn rate.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

In entrepreneurship, cash flow determines survival. This tool helps founders instantly visualize:

* Total cash on hand
* Monthly burn rate
* Remaining runway in months
* Estimated days until zero cash
* Financial health zones (Safe / Moderate / Warning / Danger)

The application emphasizes both functionality and polished user experience, transforming a simple calculation into an actionable founder dashboard.

# 🎨 Design Choices

## Why futuristic UI?

The goal was to make the product feel like a premium startup intelligence dashboard rather than a basic calculator.

### Design priorities:

* Strong visual urgency in danger states
* Reassuring visuals in safe states
* Immediate clarity for founders
* High contrast for readability
* Interactive and engaging presentation

### Examples:

* Green = Stability
* Yellow = Caution
* Orange = Warning
* Red = Critical danger

This approach improves emotional clarity and user decision-making.

# 📊 Calculation Logic

```txt
Runway (Months) = Total Cash on Hand / Monthly Burn Rate
Runway (Days) = Runway Months × 30
```
